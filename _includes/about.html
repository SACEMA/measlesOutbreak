About

SACEMA has developed a mathematical model to estimate the number of cases prevented by vaccination in a closed population, such as a school. Clicking on the icon at the top of the page will allow you to play with the model.

This page was created in response to the measles outbreak in Stellenbosch in Jan-Feb 2017 by Juliet Pulliam and James Azam. Juliet is the Director of SACEMA, and James is a postgraduate student in Mathematics.
